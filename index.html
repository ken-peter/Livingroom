Welcome to The Living Room

Where Culinary Artistry Meets Sophistication.

Our Menu Book a Table

In the prestigious Kimihurura district of Kigali, THE LIVING ROOM is now open!


This upscale and inviting restaurant lounge concept extends a warm welcome within its exceptionally comfortable lounge area. Guests can indulge in the culinary experience while enjoying the scenic views from the restaurant and private balconies, overlooking the captivating Land of a Thousand Hills. THE LIVING ROOM presents an exquisite culinary journey featuring gourmet and bistronomic cuisine, showcasing four distinctive flavors curated to delight your palate:

  • French Cuisine.
  • Italian Cuisine.
  • African Delicacies.
  • Thai Specials.

Complementing the culinary offerings, a well-appointed cocktail bar awaits, featuring an extensive selection of precious wines and whiskies. Immerse yourself in the delightful ambiance, ensuring a return visit after a splendid evening filled with great vibes and relaxation!

Why Us

Why Choose Our Restaurant

01

Passion for Gastronomy

We are driven by an unyielding passion for the art of gastronomy. Our chefs meticulously craft each dish, blending creativity and tradition to present a menu that is a symphony of flavors.

02

Uncompromising Quality

We believe in the uncompromising pursuit of quality ingredients. From locally sourced produce to premium imports, every element on your plate is selected with care, ensuring a dining experience that consistently exceeds expectations.

Community Connection

We are more than just a dining destination; we are a community hub. We take pride in being an integral part of the local community, fostering connections and contributing positively to the vibrant tapestry of our neighborhood.
